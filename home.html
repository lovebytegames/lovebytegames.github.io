FAQ

How long does a logo take?

I can take just over an hour or more to create a logo, it only depends on the complexity and revisions.

How much does a logo cost?

The price depends on the design elements and the time required.
An hour or more of work is a minimum of 25 GBP.

Can I request changes?

Yes, revisions are free, if you're not satisfied with the logo, I will work with you until you are happy with the final design.

How do I pay?

Payment can be made via PayPal. I will provide you with my PayPal username once we agree on the logo details and price.

What files do I receive?

You will recieve a high-resolution PNG with a transparent background in a zip file to prevent Discord compression.
